About

Here are somethings about us.

Gotta Go is a startup company eliminating the major problem of always ending up in terrible restrooms. We solved that problem by creating an app that lets our dedicated users contribute to the community by reviewing that restrooms they go to. Much like Google Reviews, this allows others to know the quality of the restroom without actually being there. This saves everyone time and effort. Our approach gave our users a streamlined experience in finding the best and nearest restrooms around.
